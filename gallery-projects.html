Photos by Jenny Kendler
In collaboration with Gallery 400 , UIC's on campus gallery, and environmental artist Jenny Kendler, "Garden for a Changing Climate is a community-driven participatory public art project that uses a traveling garden of local plants to give Chicagoans a dynamic and tangible experience of the central effects of climate change". This city wide project stopped by the UIC Quad on April 23, one day after Earth day. After organizing with Luis, a student worker at The UIC Greenhouse, he and I gave out over 100 potted plants to happy new plant parents!
