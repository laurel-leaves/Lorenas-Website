About Me



I live in Chicago, Illinois with my cat, Marley. In 2019 I graduated from University of Illinois at Chicago, cum laude, with a Bachelor's Degree in Integrated Design and the Arts, now know as Interdisciplinary Education in the Arts (IDEAs) . This degree jump started my path into the field of New Media. New media incorporates everything I enjoy about art, design and technology. My focus has shifted towards New Media, though the studio is always a place where I feel equally as comfortable. I love to travel! So far I have been to Mexico, Italy, Poland, and Ecuador. I learn so much about myself and other cultures throughout these trips and I only hope to continue expanding. Thank you for reading <3
