About Me



I live in Chicago, Illinois with my cat, Marley With a solid background in studio art, my focus has been mainly in the field of New Media. In 2019 I graduated from University of Illinois at Chicago, cum laude, with a Bachelor's Degree in Integrated Design and the Arts, now know as Interdisciplinary Education in the Arts (IDEAs) I love to travel! So far I have been to Mexico, Italy, Poland, and Ecuador. I learn so much about myself throughout these trips and I only hope to continue traveling.
