Lorena Hernandez

Portfolio | About


Lorena is a Chicago based artist with a passion to create. She specializes in the studio arts, mainly drawing with pencil and charcoal, as well as linocut printmaking.

She is a graduate from University of Illinois at Chicago where she earned a Bachelor's Degree in Integrated Design and the Arts, known as Interdisciplinary Education in the Arts (IDEAs) . This degree jump started her path into the fields of design and new media art. Human-centered design is at the very core of Lorena's principles, as well as a deep empathetic view of the world. She strives to add compassion, understanding, and a child-like curiousity to the design process.

When she is not working on her drawings or creative coding projects, you can find her delving into the other arts of music (current song on-repeat: Teachers by Daft Punk), film (last watched: My Left foot), and literature (current book: Dune by Frank Herbert). Having been to Mexico, Italy, Poland, and Ecuador, traveling is a big part of who Lorena is, and she hopes the keep adding to the list!
