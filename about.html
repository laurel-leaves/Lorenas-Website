About Me



I live in Chicago, Illinois with my cat, Marley. In 2019 I graduated from University of Illinois at Chicago, cum laude, with a Bachelor's Degree in Integrated Design and the Arts, now know as Interdisciplinary Education in the Arts (IDEAs) . This degree jump started my path into the field of New Media. New media incorporates everything I love about art, design, and technology.

I love to travel and dive into other cultures! So far I have been to Mexico, Italy, Poland, and Ecuador. During these trips I learn about myself, and the world around me. I hope to continue expanding and evolving, personally and professionally.

This website is made 100% by me, using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. All of the art is made by me as well.
