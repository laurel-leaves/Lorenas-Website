About Me

I live in Chicago, Illinois with my cat, Marley. My passion is creating. Designing, drawing, sculpting, building, painting, coding, I like it all.

In 2019 I graduated from University of Illinois at Chicago, cum laude, with a Bachelor's Degree in Integrated Design and the Arts, known as Interdisciplinary Education in the Arts (IDEAs) . This degree jump started my path into the fields of human centered design and new media, which incorporate everything I love about art and technology.

I love to travel and dive into other cultures! So far I have been to Mexico, Italy, Poland, and Ecuador. Throughout these trips I learn about myself, and the world around me. I hope to continue expanding and evolving, personally and professionally.

This website is made 100% from scratch, by me, using HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
